Frequently Addressed Inquiries

What are the restricted batch sizes?
We firmly cap our attendance at 10-15 students per batch. This is a non-negotiable rule to preserve the integrity of individual attention during our sessions.
Is official study material provided?
Yes. Registered students receive formalized workbooks, formula compilations, and exclusive question banks drafted by our senior educators.
How is parent communication handled?
We believe in complete formal transparency. Parents receive structured monthly reports regarding examination scores and punctuality, along with directly scheduled parent-faculty meetings.

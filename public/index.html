Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining.

How many students are there in a single batch?
We strictly maintain small batch sizes of 10-15 students. This allows our expert teachers to provide individual attention and address specific doubts immediately.
Do you provide study materials and notes?
Yes, we provide comprehensive, expertly crafted study materials, formula sheets, and chapter-wise question banks tailored to the specific board syllabus.
How often are tests conducted?
We conduct weekly revision tests on topics covered during that week. Additionally, we hold monthly cumulative mock exams to prepare students for school tests.
How do you track and report progress to parents?
Parents receive detailed SMS/WhatsApp reports after every major test. We also schedule brief parent-teacher meetings once a month to discuss the child's academic trajectory.

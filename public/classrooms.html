Our Iconic Classrooms

Named after India's greatest leaders — each classroom embodies inspiration, wisdom, and excellence

Dr. Radhakrishnan Classroom

Dr. Radhakrishnan Classroom

The Classroom of Wisdom

Named after Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan — the philosopher-president who championed education and the teaching profession. This classroom symbolizes academic wisdom, intellectual depth, and the belief that teachers are the true builders of the nation.

  • Academic wisdom & teaching excellence
  • Right ambient for focused learning
  • Committed teaching of excellence
  • Detailed explanations with individual attention
🚀

Dr. Abdul Kalam Classroom

Dr. Abdul Kalam Classroom

The Classroom of Innovation

Named after Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam — the Missile Man of India and beloved former President who inspired millions of students to dream big. This classroom embodies the spirit of science, perseverance, and student inspiration.

  • Direct interactive coaching
  • Board questions practice
  • Weekly revision tests
  • Pre-board exam preparation
🕊️

MK Gandhi Classroom

MK Gandhi Classroom

The Classroom of Character

Named after Mahatma Gandhi — the father of the nation who proved that character, self-discipline, and inner strength can change the world. This classroom focuses on building the complete student — not just academically, but as a person.

  • Build self-confidence
  • Self-initiated learning
  • Celebrate talents & develop skills
  • Continuous coaching on study holidays
